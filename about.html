Harnessing Data for Healthier Crops

Published on September 18, 2025

At France Farms, we believe that the best products start with smart farming. We're currently collecting data on soil moisture, nutrient levels, and even weather patterns to create an optimal environment for our mangoes and herbs. It's a challenging but rewarding process that's helping us move closer to our goal of providing you with the healthiest, most flavorful produce possible.

For example, by monitoring soil moisture with sensors, we can precisely control our irrigation, preventing both water waste and root rot. This isn't just about efficiency; it's about nurturing our plants so they can thrive naturally, reducing the need for chemical interventions.

We're excited to see the results this growing season and will continue to share our findings with you here on the blog!
